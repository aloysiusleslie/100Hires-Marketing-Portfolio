## Post 1

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/samdunning_i-made-4-founders-doing-nr-7m-arr-combined-activity-7472678199991226368-jQ2T

### Summary
Sam Dunning shares lessons from working with four SaaS founders generating a combined £7M+ ARR. He highlights common patterns among successful founders, including a strong focus on customer problems, consistent execution, clear positioning, and disciplined prioritization. Rather than chasing every new tactic, these founders concentrate on activities that directly contribute to growth and customer value.

### Key Insight
High-growth founders succeed by maintaining focus on customer needs and consistently executing proven strategies rather than constantly pursuing new trends or distractions.

## Post 2

Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/samdunning_old-way-of-seo-traffic-at-all-costs-the-activity-7471634809656778752-JL_7

### Summary
Sam Dunning contrasts the traditional SEO approach of maximizing website traffic at all costs with a more modern, revenue-focused strategy. He argues that generating large volumes of traffic is meaningless if it does not attract qualified buyers or contribute to pipeline growth. Instead, businesses should focus on creating content that addresses customer pain points, targets high-intent search queries, and supports broader demand generation efforts.

### Key Insight
The goal of SEO is not traffic alone—it is attracting the right audience and generating measurable business outcomes such as leads, pipeline, and revenue.
