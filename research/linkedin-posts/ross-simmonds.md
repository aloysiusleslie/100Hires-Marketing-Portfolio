Link:

### Summary
Ross Simmonds discusses Reddit as an underutilized marketing channel for brands. He explains that many companies focus heavily on traditional social media platforms while overlooking Reddit's highly engaged communities. By understanding subreddit culture, providing genuine value, and participating authentically, brands can build trust, gather customer insights, and generate meaningful engagement.

### Key Insight
Successful Reddit marketing is driven by authenticity and community participation rather than promotional messaging. Brands that contribute value first are more likely to earn attention and trust.

## Post 2

Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_youtube-videomarketing-aeo-activity-7472653370965798912-THDN

### Summary
Ross Simmonds discusses the growing importance of YouTube in the era of AI-powered search and answer engines. He explains that video content is becoming increasingly valuable because search engines and AI systems frequently surface YouTube content when answering user questions. Brands that consistently create educational and searchable videos can increase their visibility, authority, and long-term discoverability.

### Key Insight
YouTube is no longer just a social platform—it is becoming a critical search and discovery channel. Creating high-quality educational videos can improve both brand awareness and AI-era visibility.
