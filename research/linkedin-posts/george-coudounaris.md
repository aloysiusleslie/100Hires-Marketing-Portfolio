### Summary
George Coudounaris highlights a common mistake made by outbound sales teams: focusing too heavily on volume rather than relevance. He explains that many teams prioritize sending more messages instead of improving targeting, personalization, and understanding customer pain points. Effective outbound campaigns require a deep understanding of the audience and messaging that resonates with their specific challenges.

### Key Insight
Outbound success is driven more by relevance and customer understanding than by the number of messages sent.

## Post 2

Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/b2b-marketing-expert_b2bmarketing-demandgeneration-activity-7467704103398318080-lwfj

### Summary
George Coudounaris discusses the importance of demand generation in modern B2B marketing. He explains that many companies focus too heavily on capturing existing demand while neglecting activities that create future demand. Building awareness, educating the market, and consistently delivering valuable content help organizations stay top-of-mind when buyers are ready to purchase.

### Key Insight
Companies that invest in creating demand today are more likely to generate sustainable pipeline growth in the future than those relying solely on demand capture tactics.
