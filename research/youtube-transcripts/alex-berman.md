### Summary

Alex Berman demonstrates a process for finding business contact information directly from Google Maps listings. He explains how automation tools can reduce manual research time and help sales teams create larger prospect databases more efficiently.

### Key Insights

1. Google Maps can be used as a valuable source of business leads.
2. Automating lead research saves significant time compared to manual collection.
3. Organized lead databases improve outbound sales efficiency.
4. Data quality is more important than collecting large quantities of contacts.
5. Lead generation systems should support repeatable and scalable outreach processes.
